Usage guidelines

Bobo is made to be remixed.

Keep it creative, transparent, and community-minded. These simple rules protect the character without getting in the way of the meme.

Community use

✓ Good uses

  • Memes, reaction posts, and social edits
  • Fan art, animations, videos, and community graphics
  • Bobo-related thumbnails, posters, and promotional creations
  • Transformative, original work that gives Bobo a new context

× Please don’t

  • Impersonate an official Bobo account or misrepresent affiliation
  • Use Bobo assets in scams or deceptive promotions
  • Resell the raw files or repackage the media kit as your own
  • Use the character for hateful, illegal, or deliberately harmful content

These community guidelines are a plain-language usage summary, not a transfer of ownership or trademark rights.
