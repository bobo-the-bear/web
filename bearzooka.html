Distribute

Airdrop engine

Deposit any SPL token and BOBO™ software splits it evenly across your chosen recipients, building, batching, and submitting every transfer transaction to the Solana blockchain for processing.

Target

Holder targeting

Point the software at any Solana coin and it indexes that token's holder list on-chain, ranks wallets by balance, and targets the Top 10 through Top 1000 — with manual exclusions for pools and exchanges.

Monitor

Dashboard & wallet checker

Live price, market cap, liquidity, and volume from on-chain trading data — plus a checker that verifies any wallet's holdings directly against the blockchain.

Software in your browser. Transactions on the blockchain.

BOBO™ software is hosted online and accessed through this website. There is nothing to download, install, or update — every feature runs as a service, in your browser, against the Solana network. Your tokens move only when you approve each batch in your own wallet.

  1. Connect. Link any Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, Backpack). Your keys never leave it.
  2. Configure. Pick the token and amount to distribute, then the target coin and how deep into its holder list to reach.
  3. Review. The software fetches the holder list on-chain and previews every recipient, the per-wallet amount, and estimated network cost.
  4. Send. Approve the batched transactions in your wallet and BOBO™ software processes the distribution on the Solana blockchain, confirming each transfer live.
